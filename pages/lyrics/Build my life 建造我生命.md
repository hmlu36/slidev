# Build my life

---

#### [Verse 1]
# 配得 我們獻上每一首歌曲
# 配得 我們所有讚美的聲音

## Worthy of every song we could ever sing

## We live for You

## Jesus the name above every other name

## Worthy of every breath we could ever breathe
## We live for You

## And fill me with Your heart
